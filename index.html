Welcome to Coballo
Let's Get You Started!

Are you a software engineer or developer? Are you into tech? Have you worked on projects to the points that you got tired of the projects and preferred givig up on them? If you had other developers willing to help you complete the unfinished project, would you let them? If you saw an interesting but unfinished project would you be happy to collaborate with the author? If your answer to any of the questions aforementioned is yes then you are in the right place.
Coballo is an open source website geared towards assisting developers who are stuck on a project or who are willing to assist developers who are stuck on a project to ensure that ideas are not just abandoned as unfinished projects on github. We connect you to github to either share your unfinished projects or to assist a stuck developer finish a project. If you are interested then click the button below.
